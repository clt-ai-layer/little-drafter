### Context & Findings

- **Feature**: `little-drafter` (Guided Drawing for 4-year-olds).
- **Core Pattern**: Paper-based drawing guided by an SVG-revealing application.
- **Questions**: 8 questions across 4 sections (Guidance Mechanics, Domain & State, Content & Assets, UX & Accessibility).
- **Answers**: Recommended answers focus on high-contrast visual cues (blue pulsing lines), clear navigation (large arrows), and TTS support.

    - `DrawingCompleted` event will trigger rewards and persistence.
